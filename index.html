Job Teaser Creator

Welcome to the Job Teaser Creator. This tool helps you prepare job information for AI-assisted teaser creation. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter the job ad URL and edit the company logo URL if needed
  2. Paste the full job ad into the form
  3. Review and edit the Job Teaser Template if needed
  4. Click "Prepare for AI" to format the content
  5. Use the "Copy to Clipboard" button to copy the prepared content
  6. Click on ChatGPT or Claude button to open your preferred AI tool
  7. Paste the copied content into the AI tool to generate your job teaser
